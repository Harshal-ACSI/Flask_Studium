Welcome to Biology!

Definition

Biology is the natural science that studies life and living organisms, including their physical structure, chemical processes, molecular interactions, physiological mechanisms, development, and evolution.

History

The history of biology traces back to ancient times when scholars began exploring the living world. Early naturalists meticulously observed and classified organisms, paving the way for modern biological sciences. Key milestones include the invention of the microscope, the discovery of cells, and the formulation of the theory of evolution by natural selection.

Applications

Studying biology helps us understand our environment, improve human health, and develop solutions for global challenges. Advances in biology contribute to medical innovations, conservation efforts, sustainable agriculture, and understanding the impact of humans on ecosystems.
